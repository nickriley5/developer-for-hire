Since beginning my Jiu-Jitsu journey 4 years ago, so many things in my life have changed. I started just wanting to lose my "dad bod" and ended up getting in the best shape of my life. Recently, God blessed us with opening our own Gym with my best friend, Micah Scarbrough. Our passion is helping individuals achieve whatever goal they personally have.
